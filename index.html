JDesign

J Design was founded in 2012 and is based deep in the creative community of Bristol, England. I am a freelance graphic designer looking to show a fresh perspective on the world through empowering and expansive design.

I have been producing art for 20 years and I have been using computers for almost as long so it was a clear choice to combine the two and create a Graphic Design business focused on collaborating closely with clients to produce powerful and potent graphics and websites for professional use.
